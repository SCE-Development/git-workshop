Midnight Peanut Noodles

It’s 11:47pm and you suddenly remember you’re a person who needs food.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz noodles (ramen, udon, spaghetti—no judgment)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional but extremely main-character)
  • 1–2 tsp sriracha (optional)
  • Splash of hot noodle water (the secret handshake)
  • Toppings: scallions, sesame seeds, crushed peanuts

Steps

  1. Cook noodles. Save ~1/4 cup of hot noodle water.
  2. Mix peanut butter, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and sriracha in a bowl.
  3. Add a splash of noodle water and stir until glossy and sauce-like.
  4. Toss noodles with sauce. Add more noodle water if needed.
  5. Top with whatever makes you feel powerful.
