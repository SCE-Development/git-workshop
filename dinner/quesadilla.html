Chicken Quesadilla

items

  • tortillas
  • chicken
  • cheese
  • jalapeno peppers

steps

  • shred the cooked chicken
  • place cheese, chicken, and jalapenos on a tortilla
  • fold the tortilla in half
  • toast in a pan until the cheese is melted
  • cut into slices and serve

food critic

needs a bit more melted cheese on the edges
