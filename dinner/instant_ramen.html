Instant Ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 package instant ramen noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Green onions (optional)
  • Seasoning packet

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for 3 minutes
  3. Add the seasoning packet and stir
  4. Optional: crack an egg into the pot and stir
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
