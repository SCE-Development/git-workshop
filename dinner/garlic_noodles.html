Midnight Garlic Noodles

A classic recipe you can make quickly

Ingredients

  • 1 bundle noodles (ramen, spaghetti, udon—anything)
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until done; save a splash of noodle water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter; cook garlic 30–60 seconds.
  3. Add soy sauce + sugar + chili flakes.
  4. Toss noodles in sauce, add noodle water if needed.
  5. Eat immediately, preferably standing at the counter.
