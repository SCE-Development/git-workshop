Simple Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp soysauce
  • 2 sliced green onions
  • pinch of black pepper

Directions

  1. Scramble the eggs
  2. Saute garlic with oil
  3. Stir fry vegetables for 1-2 mins
  4. Fry the rice on high for 3-4 mins
  5. Pour soy sauce and season with black pepper
  6. Fold scrambled eggs in and stir
