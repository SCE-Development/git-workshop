Texas BBQ Beef Brisket Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8lb whole beef brisket
  • ½ cup of coarse kosher salt
  • ½ cup of coarse black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • A full chimney of charcoal briquettes
  • A few chunks of post oak wood

Instructions:

  1. mix the salt, black pepper, and garlic powder into a mixture and rub onto the brisket
  2. light the charcoal briquettes in a chimney starter for 20 min
  3. pour hot charcoal into smoker and add post oak wood chunks
  4. set smoker to 225°F
  5. place brisket fat-side up on the grill grates and close the lid
  6. smoke the brisket until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (typically 6–8 hours)
  7. remove the brisket from the smoker and let it rest for at least 1 hour
  8. slice the brisket against the grain into ¼-inch thick pieces for serving and enjoy!
