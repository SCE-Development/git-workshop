Banana Cream Pie

No-bake. No drama. All banana.

Prep
20 min
Chill
2 hrs
Serves
8
Difficulty
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made graham cracker crust
  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1 box (3.4 oz) instant vanilla pudding
  • 2 cups cold whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk the instant vanilla pudding mix with 2 cups of cold milk for about 2 minutes until thickened. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Layer the sliced bananas in a single layer across the bottom of the graham cracker crust.
  3. Pour the pudding evenly over the banana layer, smoothing it to the edges.
  4. In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until stiff peaks form.
  5. Spread or pipe the whipped cream over the pudding layer.
  6. Refrigerate uncovered for at least 2 hours (or overnight for best results) before slicing and serving.

Tips

🍌 Bananas browning? Toss slices in a little lemon juice before layering — it keeps them looking fresh.

🥧 Extra crunch? Crush a few vanilla wafers on top right before serving.

⏱ Make ahead: This pie is even better the next day once everything melds together in the fridge.
