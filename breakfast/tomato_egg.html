Tomato Egg

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tomato

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the tomato
  2. Add tomato,soy sauce, and fish sauce to pan on medium heat
  3. Cook till tender
  4. Move the tomatoes to one side of the pan, and add srambled egg to the other size
  5. Cook the egg until mostly done and then mix with tomatoes
  6. Finish cooking it
  7. Serve over rice!
