Pancakes

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter/li>
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Mix wet ingredients
  3. Combine wet + dry
  4. Heat pan on medium
  5. Pour batter
  6. Flip when bubbles form
  7. Cook other side
  8. Serve warm
