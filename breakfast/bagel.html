Bagel

A simple (but delicious) breakfast, especially if you're in a hurry.

Ingredients:

  • Bread Buns
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Cream Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cut a hole in two buns
  2. Toast them!
  3. Spread cream cheese on one bun
  4. Place the two buns together
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top (optional)
  6. Enjoy!

Food Critic Review

  • Incredibly portable breakfast.
  • Hole in bagel allows ventilation to prevent overheating.
  • Cream cheese acts as thermal paste.
