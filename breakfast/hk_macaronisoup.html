Hong Kong Style Macaroni Soup

Clean and simple breakfast to get your day started

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • 1-2 slices of ham
  • Chicken broth or water with chicken bouillon
  • Salt

Instructions:

  • Boil macaroni until soft
  • Heat broth separately
  • Add ham into broth for about a minute
  • Add macaroni in
  • Season with salt if needed
