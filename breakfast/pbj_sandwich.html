PB and J sandwich

A simple (but delicious) breakfast, especially if you're in a hurry.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jam
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions:

  1. Toast your slices of bread
  2. Lay them out
  3. Spread peanut butter on one of the slices
  4. Spread the strawberry jam on the other slice
  5. Put the slices together, sides of the condiments should be touching
  6. Enjoy!
