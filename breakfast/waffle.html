Protein Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup Kodiak Cakes Waffle Mix
  • 2/3 cup water (plus or minus 1/8 cup water)
  • 1/8 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • Maple Syrup

Instructions:

  1. Mix waffle mix, blueberries, salt, and water together until mixture is of a paste-like consistency. Add more or less water to adjust consistency as needed.
  2. If needed, add more water to dissolve chunks of waffle mix.
  3. Prepare in waffle maker for about 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  4. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy!
Food Critic Review: 4.5/5
