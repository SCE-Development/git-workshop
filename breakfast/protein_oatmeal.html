Protein Oatmeal (overnight)

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Frozen fruits (preferably berries)
  • Protein Powder

Steps:

  1. Pour oats and milk into a bowl and mix. About 1:1 ratio.
  2. Put as many frozen fruits as you like.
  3. Put in fridge overnight
  4. Take out the bowl in the morning, put in a scoop of protein powder, and mix.
