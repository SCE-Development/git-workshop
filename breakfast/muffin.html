Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mix-ins (chocolate chips or blueberries)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Line a muffin tin with paper liners or grease it lightly.
  3. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  4. In another bowl, mix milk, oil or butter, egg, and vanilla.
  5. Combine wet ingredients with dry ingredients until just mixed.
  6. Fold in the mix-ins.
  7. Fill muffin cups evenly with batter.
  8. Bake for 18 to 22 minutes.
