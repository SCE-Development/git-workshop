Chinese Sausage Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Day-Old rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 (30 ml) tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 1 (15 ml) tablespoon Maggi
  • 1 ½ (23 ml) tablespoon Sesame Oil
  • 2 Chinese Sausages
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Green Onions
  • White Pepper
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the onions into small pieces and slice the green onions in half.
  2. Chop the white parts of the green onions into thirds and the green parts into small slices.
  3. Sauté onions and the white portions of the green onions until soft and translucent. Remove from the wok.
  4. Cook the Chinese sausages and remove from the wok.
  5. Mix and cook the eggs in the wok until mostly cooked through, then remove.
  6. Add the day-old rice and cook until warmed through.
  7. Add the soy sauce, Maggi, and sesame oil.
  8. Mix in the eggs, sausage, sautéed onions, and green onions.
  9. Season with white pepper.
  10. Serve in a bowl and garnish with the green slices of green onion.
