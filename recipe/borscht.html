Best Borscht Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 Beets
  • 4 TBSP olive oil
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 yukon potatoes
  • 1lb beef chuck roast

Instructions:

  1. Dice potatoes and slice carrots thinly, shred or cube beets
  2. Brown beef with a little oil
  3. Throw everything into the pot, simmer till its done
