One-Pot Butter Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 serving spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 big cheese
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water, drain until there only a little bit of pasta water left.
  2. In the same pot, melt butter and saute briefly.
  3. Toss pasta back in, mix well, season three times, cheesemax and serve.
