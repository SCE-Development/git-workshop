Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Butter
  • Jelly or Jam
  • 2 Slices of White Bread

Steps:

  1. Butter the Bread First
  2. Toast Both Pieces of bread
  3. Apply Jelly or Jam to One Piece of Bread
  4. Apply Peanut Butter to the other slice of bread
  5. serve as soon as possible to avoid sogginess
