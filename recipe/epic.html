Butter Toast

Incredibly Important Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • A whole 3 lb bag of sugar

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Spread butter on the warm toast.
  3. Dip the toast right into the bag of sugar
  4. Repeat the last step until every single hole of the bread is filled with sugar.
  5. Serve immediately.
