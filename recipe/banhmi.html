TOP 1 BANHMI

Ingredients:

  • 1 Baguette
  • 10 lbs of your choice of cooked meat
  • 50 jalapeno, sliced
  • 1 TBSP patte
  • 1 TBSP butter
  • 1 bundle parsley
  • Pickled daikon and carrots, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Slice the baguette the long way
  2. Spread the patte on one slice and the butter on the other
  3. Assemble you BanhMi with parsley, pickled veggies, jalapenos, and your choice of meat
  4. Enjoy!
