Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Splash of milk
  • Salt & pepper
  • Sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Crack egg into a microwave-safe mug.
  2. Add a splash of milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add a touch of sesame oil
  4. Whisk with a fork.
  5. Microwave for ~1 minute, stir halfway. Done!
