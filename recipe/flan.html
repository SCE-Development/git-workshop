Flan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 can evaporated milk (12 oz)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  • Pour sugar and water into saucepan and heat until golden = carmel
  • Pour caramel into a baking dish
  • Whisk eggs with milk and vanilla = custard
  • Pour custard over caramel
  • Bake in water at 350°F ~50–60 mins look for wobbly center
  • Cool it down in fridge for 4hrs+
