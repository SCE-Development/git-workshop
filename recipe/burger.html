Juicy Classic Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoons of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Instructions:

  1. Form beef into a patty, season with salt and pepper
  2. Cook on medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes each side
  3. Add cheese during last minute of cooking
  4. Melt the butter in a skillet, then brush it generously on the cut sides of the buns before toasting them in the pan
  5. Assemble burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments

Food Critic Review

Very tasty, although I prefer the onions grilled.
