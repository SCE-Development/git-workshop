Salad to get Shredded

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix mayo, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire, garlic, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  2. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
