Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Pour oats, water, and salt into a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute then stir slowly.
  3. Repeat microwaving and stirring every 30 seconds for 2 more minutes.
  4. Let it cool for 1 minute.
