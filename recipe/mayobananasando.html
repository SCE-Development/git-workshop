Mayo Banana Sando

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • ice as needed

Instructions:

  1. Peel the banana and cut it into slices.
  2. Spread the mayonnaise on the bread.
  3. Place the banana slices on the bread.
  4. Spread the butter on the bread.
  5. Toast the bread in a toaster.
  6. Serve the sandwich with a side of ice.
