Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients

  • ½ cup Greek yogurt or any yogurt you like
  • ¼ cup granola
  • ½ cup fresh fruit (e.g., berries, banana slices, mango)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup

Instructions

  • Use a spoon to add half the yogurt into a glass or bowl.
  • Add a layer of fruit and granola.
  • Add the rest of the yogurt.
  • Top with more granola and drizzle with honey or syrup.
