Popcorn Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken thighs (cut small)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 egg
  • Sweet potato starch (or cornstarch)
  • Salt & white/black pepper
  • Basil leaves
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce + garlic (30 min)
  2. Add egg, then coat with starch
  3. Fry in hot oil until golden (4 to 5 mins)
  4. Sprinkle with salt & pepper
