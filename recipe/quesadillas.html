Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 oz of cheese
  • 1 tbsp of avocado oil
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the tortilla to the pan and cook for 1 minute on each side.
  3. Add the cheese to the tortilla and cook for 1 minute on each side.
  4. Add the salsa, sour cream, and guacamole to the tortilla and cook for 1 minute on each side.
  5. Fold the tortilla in half and enjoy!
