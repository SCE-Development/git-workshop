Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

  • Broth
  • Noodles
  • Aromatics
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Put noodles into water
  3. Fry aromatics in oil in another pot
  4. Pour in broth into aromatics pot
  5. Drain noodles when cooked, cool by running cold water thorugh it
  6. Serve broth and noods
