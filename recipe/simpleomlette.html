Simple Omelette

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • A teaspoon of red chili powder
  • A teaspoon of salt
  • Slice of bread
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Crack egg into bowl
  2. Put teaspoon of red chili powder into same bowl
  3. Put teaspoon of salt into same bowl
  4. Mix until everything is fully mixed together
  5. Pour cooking oil and then pour the egg mix into pan
  6. Cook both sides of omlette to your liking
  7. Toast the bread in toaster
  8. Eat

Food Critic Review

This recipe is quick and easy to make, tastes good, and has good nutrition. So a good choice for breakfast.
