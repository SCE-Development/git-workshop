Bibimbap

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of steamed rice
  • 1 cup assorted vegetables (carrots, zucchini, spinach, bean sprouts)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Bulgogi beef or tofu

Steps

  1. Prepare steamed rice and place it in a large bowl.
  2. Saut√© or blanch each vegetable separately, season lightly with salt or garlic.
  3. Cook bulgogi beef or tofu with soy sauce, garlic, and a little sugar.
  4. Fry an egg sunny-side-up.
  5. Neatly arrange the vegetables and protein over the rice.
  6. Top with the egg, drizzle sesame oil, and add a spoonful of gochujang.
  7. Mix everything together and enjoy!
