Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • 0.75 banana
  • 2 Bacon strips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and toast it.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one or both slices.
  3. Slice banana and layer it in.
  4. If you wanna feel like Elvis, put some bacon in that thing!
  5. Close, slice, and yum!
