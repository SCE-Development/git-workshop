Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • 0.75 banana

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread and toast it.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one or both slices.
  3. Slice banana and layer it in.
  4. Close, slice, and yum!
