Thai Milk Tea

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Thai tea leaves or 2 tea bags
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp evaporated milk
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Steep the Thai tea in hot water for 5 minutes.
  2. Strain out the tea leaves or remove tea bags.
  3. Mix in sweetened condensed milk while hot.
  4. Let it cool slightly and pour over ice.
