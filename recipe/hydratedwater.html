Hydrated Water

Ingredients:

  • Dehydrated Water
  • Water
  • Some more water
  • Some liquified dehydrated water

Instructions:

  1. Put 5g dehydrated water in a glass.
  2. Be careful not to have more of it or it could start tasting too "watery"
  3. Keep adding until its runny
  4. Add precisely 376ml of water
  5. Add the remaining water after stirring continuously and you're done!!
  6. Optionally garnish with a sprinkle of water
