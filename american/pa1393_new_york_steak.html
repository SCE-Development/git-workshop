How to cook a New-York Steak

Ingredients

  • Ingredient 1: A strip of New-York Steak
  • Ingredient 2: Butter
  • Ingredient 3: Salt
  • Ingredient 4: Pepper

Steps

  1. Step one: Season the steak with salt and pepper. Rub salt pepper on both sides of the steak.
  2. Step two: Add butter to the pan and start to heat it up until it starts sizzling.
  3. Step three: Put the steak on, one side for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Step three: Flip it over, other side for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Step four: Enjoy!
