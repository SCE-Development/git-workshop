Shrimp and Grits Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup stone-ground grits
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring water and milk to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Slowly whisk in the grits and reduce heat to low.
  3. Cook grits for 20â€“25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thick and creamy.
  4. Stir in butter and shredded cheese until melted.
  5. In a skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  6. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
  7. Add shrimp, paprika, pepper, and salt, then cook until shrimp are pink.
  8. Spoon grits into bowls and top with shrimp and bacon.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
