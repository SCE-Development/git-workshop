Buffalo Chicken Wrap Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grilled cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup ranch
  • 2 medium flour tortillas
  • 1/4 diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked chicken and buffalo sauce until fully coated.
  2. Lay out flour tortillas and divide chicken evenly among them.
  3. Sprinkle your lettuce onto the chicken.
  4. Take your diced tomatoes and onion and put on top of your shredduce.
  5. Sprinkle as much as shredded cheese as you like on top.
  6. Pour your ranch onto your vegetables.
  7. Fold the sides of the tortilla in and roll the wrap burrito=style
  8. Done and ready to eat!
