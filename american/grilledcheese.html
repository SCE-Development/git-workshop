Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between unbuttered sides.
  3. Cook on a pan until golden on both sides.

Food Critic Review

A classic sandwich - a perfect combination of bread, cheese, butter, and heat. Simplicity at its finest.
