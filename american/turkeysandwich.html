Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 3 slices turkey breast
  • 1 slice cheese
  • 1 tablespoon pesto

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Spread pesto on one slice of bread.
  3. Place turkey slices and cheese between the slices of bread.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
