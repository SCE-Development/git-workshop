Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 3 slices turkey breast
  • 1 slice cheese
  • 1 tablespoon pesto

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Spread pesto on one slice of bread.
  3. Place turkey slices and cheese between the slices of bread.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.

Food Critic Review

This turkey sandwich is a delicious and satisfying meal. The combination of the savory turkey, melted cheese, and flavorful pesto creates a perfect balance of flavors. The toasted bread adds a nice crunch to the sandwich. It's a great option for a quick lunch or a light dinner.
