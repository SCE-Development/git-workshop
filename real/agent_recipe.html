Garlic Butter Pasta

A quick, comforting pasta you can throw together in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti or any pasta
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped parsley or green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter with the olive oil.
  3. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1–2 minutes, stirring, until fragrant but not browned.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, tossing to coat in the garlic butter. Splash in a bit of reserved pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Toss in Parmesan to taste so it melts into a light sauce.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with more Parmesan and chopped parsley or green onions.
  7. Serve immediately while hot.
