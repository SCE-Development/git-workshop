yummy spam musubi recipe

ingredients

  • spam
  • marinade / glaze
  • sushi rice
  • nori seaweed
  • sesame seeds

instructions

  1. cook your rice
  2. cook and marinade your spam
  3. lay a strip of seaweed flat
  4. place a spam-shaped block of rice on the seaweed
  5. put a slice of spam on the rice
  6. sprinkle some sesame seeds on the rice for extra flavorrr
  7. wrap seaweed around the spam + rice
  8. use a little water to seal the seaweed
