Family Chicken & Potato Bake

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 3 or 4 potatoes, chopped
  • 2 TBSP olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika or oregano (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Toss potatoes with olive oil, garlic, salt, and spices
  3. Place potatoes in a baking dish and add chicken on top
  4. Season chicken well and bake for 45 or 50 minutes, turning once

Recipe Lore

This is a family-style comfort meal that always shows up when everyone wants something simple, filling, and cozy — crispy potatoes, juicy chicken, and zero stress.
