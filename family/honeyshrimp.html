Honey Shrimp

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pepper and salt
  • Egg
  • Mayonnaise
  • Condensed Milk
  • Honey
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Add pepper, salt, egg white to shrimp and mix. Let it marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, condensed milk, honey for the sauce.
  3. Refigerate sauce for 15 minutes.
  4. Add cornlllstarch to shrimp.
  5. Deep fry shrimp.
  6. Mix shrimp and sauce.
