Hot Spicy Soup Recipe !!!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 2 - 3 chillis (finely chopped)
  • 4 tbsp spring onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (finely chopped)
  • 3 tbsp cabbage (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 bell pepper (finely chopped)
  • 4 cups of water
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder

Corn Flour Slurry:

  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps:

  1. heat oil in a large pot over medium heat - stir fry the garlic, ginger and chilli
  2. add in the carrot, cabbage, bell pepper, and HALF of the chopped spring onion - stir fry until the vegetables are soft
  3. add in the soy sauce, vinegar, chilli sauce, salt and pepper powder - stir fry for 2 minutes
  4. add in the water, soy sauce, vinegar, and chilli sauce and bring to a boil
  5. stir well and add the pepper powder and salt
  6. mix the corn flour with water to make a slurry - add to the soup and stir until thickened
  7. finally, add the remaining spring onions
  8. ENJOY!
