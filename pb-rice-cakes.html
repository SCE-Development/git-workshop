Peanut Butter Rice Cakes

Ingredients

  • Rice cakes of your liking
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey if you want

Instructions

  1. Grab some rice cakes.
  2. Set them on a plate.
  3. Spread the peanut butter across the surface.
  4. Drizzle honey overtop of the peanut butter.
