Strawberry Boba

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup tapioca pearls (boba)
  • 1 cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 cups water (for boiling boba)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water. Add tapioca pearls and cook for about 20 minutes until soft and chewy.
  2. Drain pearls and rinse them under cold water.
  3. In a blender, mix strawberries, sugar, and 1/2 cup water until smooth to make strawberry syrup.
  4. Add cooked pearls to the strawberry syrup and mix well.
  5. Fill a glass with ice, pour in milk, then add matcha and strawberry boba mixture.
