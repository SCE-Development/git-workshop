Easy Berry Smoothie 🍓

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • Â½ cup vanilla greek yogurt
  • 1-2 tbsp honey or any sweetener(optional)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness (add more milk to thin or more berries to thicken).
  4. Pour and enjoy!
