Strawberry-Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Strawberries
  • Frozen Bananas
  • Fruit juice of choice
  • Protein Powder (Optional)

Instructions:

  1. Put strawberries into blender
  2. Put bananas into blender
  3. Put small amount of juice into blender
  4. Add half a scoop of protein powder
  5. Blend and add water if needed until pourable
