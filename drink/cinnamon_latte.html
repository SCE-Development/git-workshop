☕ Bhoomika's Homemade Cinnamon Latte

This is my go-to drink on cold mornings or whenever I need a little warmth. It's way better than anything from a coffee shop and takes about 10 minutes. The cinnamon makes it feel cozy without being too sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or ½ cup of strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 cup of milk (whole milk works best, but oat milk is great too)
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • A tiny pinch of nutmeg (optional but trust me)
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew your espresso or strong coffee and pour it into your favorite mug.
  2. In a small saucepan, warm the milk over medium heat — don't let it boil.
  3. Stir in the cinnamon, sugar (or honey), and nutmeg into the warm milk.
  4. Froth the milk using a frother, a whisk, or just shake it in a jar with the lid on.
  5. Pour the frothed cinnamon milk over your espresso.
  6. Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon if you're feeling fancy.
  7. Sit down, take a sip, and enjoy your moment. ☕

Makes 1 serving. Best enjoyed on a slow morning.
