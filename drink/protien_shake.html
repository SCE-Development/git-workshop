Protien Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of vanillia whey protien powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 spoon of peanut butter
  • 2 spoons greek yogurt
  • a few strawberries

Instructions

  1. Put the milk first followed up any liquidy ingreidents.
  2. Add the fruits and finally the powder.
  3. Blend until ready.
