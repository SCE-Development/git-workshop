Brown Sugar Boba

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup tapioca pearls (boba)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for syrup)
  • 4 cups water (for boiling)
  • 1 cup milk
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Boil 4 cups of water. Add tapioca pearls and cook for 20 minutes until soft.
  2. Drain the pearls and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a small pot, mix brown sugar and 1/2 cup water. Simmer until it becomes a thick syrup.
  4. Add the pearls to the syrup and stir to coat them.
  5. Pour the pearls and syrup into a glass, add ice, and fill with milk.
