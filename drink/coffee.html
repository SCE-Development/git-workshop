Classic Black Coffee

A simple, perfect cup of black coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground coffee
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Add ground coffee to your filter or French press.
  3. Pour hot water over the grounds.
  4. Let it brew for 4 minutes.
  5. Pour and enjoy!

Food Critic Review

This coffee is bold, smooth, and easy to make. A true classic that never disappoints and provides all the energy you need to keep you going.
