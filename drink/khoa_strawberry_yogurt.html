Khoa's Strawberry Yogurt Drink

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries (washed and hulled)
  • 1 cup plain or vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey (or to taste)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add strawberries to a blender
  2. Pour in yogurt and milk
  3. Add honey for sweetness
  4. Add ice cubes
  5. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy (about 30-45 seconds)
  6. Pour into glasses
  7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tip: For a thicker consistency, add more yogurt or frozen strawberries!
