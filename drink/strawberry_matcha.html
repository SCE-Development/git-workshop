Strawberry Matcha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 medium strawberries
  • 2.5 tablespoon white granulated sugar or granulated cane sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoon matcha ceremonial or everyday grade powder
  • 4 tablespoon hot water
  • 4-6 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add strawberries and sugar into a drinking class and crush or blend till they form a purée.
  2. Add ice cubes over the puréed strawberries, pour milk, and set aside.
  3. Sift the matcha powder into a small bowl and add hot water. Whisk until frothy.
  4. Pour the matcha liquid over the set aside strawberry purée and milk.
  5. Stir and enjoy!

Recipe Lore

Legend has it that this strawberry matcha recipe was discovered in a hidden cafe owned by a tiny cat with a tiny barista hat. One sip is said to grant instant joy.
