Simple Iced Coffee

Ingredients:

  • Strong brewed coffee
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk and sugar to taste.
  5. Stir well and enjoy your refreshing iced coffee!
