Matcha Tea

Ingredients:

  • Matcha powder
  • Hot water (not boiling)
  • (Optional) Sweetener

Instructions:

  1. Sift 1–2 teaspoons of matcha powder into a bowl to remove clumps.
  2. Heat water to about 175°F (80°C) hot but not boiling.
  3. Pour a small amount of water into the bowl and whisk into a smooth paste.
  4. Add the remaining water and whisk in a zig-zag motion until frothy.
  5. (Optional) Add sweetener if desired.

Optional Add-Ins:

  • Milk (for a matcha latte)
  • Honey
  • Vanilla

Alternate Instructions (Latte Style):

  1. Prepare matcha paste as above.
  2. Heat milk until warm and frothy.
  3. Pour milk over the matcha paste and stir gently.
  4. Enjoy!
