Poor Man's Scallops (Fried Banana)

Ingredients:

  • 1 large ripe plantain or banana
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 0.5 teaspoon brown sugar or salt (optional)

Kitchenware:

  • A cutting board of any size
  • A knife of any sharpness
  • A skillet or frying pan
  • A stovetop with one vacant cooking zone

Steps:

    Pre-cooking

  1. Fully peel the plantain
  2. Slice the plantain diagonally into even pieces that are 1–2 cm thick (0.5 inches in freedom units)

    3. Cooking

  3. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat
  4. Place the plantain slices in a single layer; cook for 2–3 minutes until the bottom side turns golden brown and caramelized
  5. Flip the slices over; immediately sprinkle the cinnamon (and optional brown sugar or salt) evenly over the cooked sides (the top)
  6. Fry the second side for another 2–3 minutes until tender and browned

    7. Post-cooking

  7. Remove from the pan and cool slightly before serving
  8. Make your family and friends happy, impressed, and probably still kind of hungry because 1 plaintain is honestly not that much food
  9. Do it again tomorrow!
