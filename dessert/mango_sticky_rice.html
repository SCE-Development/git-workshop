Mango Sticky Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ripe mango, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sticky rice until the water runs mostly clear, then soak it for 4 hours.
  2. Steam the soaked rice for 20 to 25 minutes, until tender and glossy.
  3. Warm coconut milk with sugar and salt in a small pan, stirring until dissolved.
  4. Pour most of the coconut mixture over the hot rice and let it rest for 10 minutes so the rice absorbs the flavor.
  5. Plate the sticky rice with sliced mango and drizzle the remaining coconut sauce on top.
  6. Finish with toasted sesame seeds if you want extra texture.
