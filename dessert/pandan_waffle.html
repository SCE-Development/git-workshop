Pandan Waffle

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp cornstarch
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup (100g) sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) coconut milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) water (or milk)
  • ⅓ cup (80ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tsp pandan extract/paste or 2-3 Tbsp pandan juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron to medium-high. Lightly oil/grease it.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, baking powder, cornstarch, salt, sugar.
  3. Mix wet ingredients in another bowl: eggs, coconut milk, water, oil, pandan, (vanilla).
  4. Combine: Pour wet into dry and whisk until just combined.
  5. Rest batter 10 minutes
  6. Cook: Pour batter into hot waffle iron (don't overfill). Cook until deep golden and crisp for 3-5 min.
  7. Cool for crispiness: Place waffles on a wire rack (not a plate) so steam doesn't soften them.

Food Critic Review

Crispy edges, soft center, and that pandan aroma is unreal. The coconut milk makes it rich without being heavy.
