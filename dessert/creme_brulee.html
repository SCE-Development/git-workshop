Crème Brûlée

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Extra sugar for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C)
  2. Heat the heavy cream in a pot until hot (don't boil).
  3. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar.
  4. Slowly pour the hot cream into the egg mixture while whisking.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract.
  6. Pour into ramekins.
  7. Place ramekins in a baking dish and fill the dish with hot water halfway up the ramekins.
  8. Bake for 35–40 minutes until the center slightly jiggles.
  9. Let cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  10. Sprinkle sugar on top and use a kitchen torch (or broiler) to melt it until golden and crispy.
