Chocolate Mug Cake

Quick and easy cake in a mug!

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in a mug
  2. Microwave for 1-2 minutes
  3. Enjoy!
