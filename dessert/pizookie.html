Pizookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Vanilla ice cream, or any flavor of your choosing, for topping
  • Optional: extra toppings like chocolate chips or M&M's

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt your butter, then mix it with your brown and white sugar until the consistency is smooth.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then continue mixing until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Incrementally mix the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  6. Add any extra toppings if you decided to use any.
  7. Spread the dough evenly in a skillet or baking dish.
  8. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the center is a little soft.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes, then add your ice cream and you're done!
